Products

Adding Products#

When you click on the “Shop” icon on the left shortcut menu, you’ll automatically land on “Products” tab where you can see a list of all your products.

Go to Shop > Products Click on “+” add button on the upper right side Enter Title, Description, Price, Discount and Images.

Click on the “File” save button on the upper right side.

Product Tags#

Here you can fill in product tags and create new Tags & Categories.

Product Options#

Here you can add Product Options such as Color, Size, Model, Etc.

Product Forms#

Here you can create a form to collect information from customers when they add this product to their cart. Learn more about using forms.

Editing Products#

Navigate to the specific product you'd like to edit and click on it. Shop > Products > Product

Deleting Products#

Navigate to the specific product you'd like to delete Shop > Products > Product Click on the "bin" delete button on the upper right side/
