Editting Pages

Once you’ve added your first page. Let’s fill it out with some interesting content.

Navigate to:Website editor > Home > Website > Pages

Click on the page you’d like to edit, you’ll see all of it’s different sections

Adding Sections#

To add a section choose from a list of pre-built sections (Advanced Sliders, Timelines, Counters, Styled Maps, Forms and much more)

important

Each section has it’s styling options based on your theme

Each section has it’s own fields and you can simply click the “add” button when you’re on the section you want to edit.

Removing Sections#

If you’d like to remove a section from a specific page go to that page from the website editor and click the close button next to the section

Rearranging Sections#

You can reaarange the sections order by hovering over the list of items in Sections three icons will appear (Copy, Move, Delete) click the up/down arrows on the right hand side of the sections names.
